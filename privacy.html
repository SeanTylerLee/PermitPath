Privacy Policy

How PermitPath handles your information.

Last Updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PermitPath ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the PermitPath mobile application ("the App") and our website. By using the App or our website, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy is provided for general informational purposes only and does not constitute legal advice. Privacy and data protection laws may vary based on your location. If you have specific legal questions, you should consult with a qualified attorney in your jurisdiction.

No Data Collection

PermitPath is designed to operate without collecting, storing, transmitting, or selling your personal data. We do not require you to create an account and we do not ask you to provide personal information (such as your name, email address, phone number, or payment information) inside the App.

Specifically, we do not intentionally collect or store:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or contact details entered into the App
  • Location history, GPS tracks, or route history associated with your identity
  • Device identifiers, advertising identifiers, or analytics profiles
  • Permit numbers, load details, or shipment information tied to you or your business

All route planning, waypoints, and navigation activity within the App are intended to run locally on your device and are not transmitted to our own servers.

Third-Party Services

The App may rely on third-party services (for example, map or navigation SDKs, app store services, or crash reporting services provided by Apple or Google) to function properly. These third parties may independently collect certain technical data from your device as part of providing their services. Their collection and use of information is governed by their own privacy policies, which we do not control.

We do not have direct access to any personal information those third parties may process, and we do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Purchases & Subscriptions

The App may offer in-app purchases or auto-renewing subscriptions (for example, a monthly subscription) that are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). When you initiate a purchase, your payment information is handled directly by Apple or Google, and we do not receive or store your full credit card number or other sensitive payment details.

We may receive limited information related to your purchase from Apple or Google (such as a transaction identifier, product purchased, country, and subscription status) so that we can activate or manage your access to paid features, address support requests, and comply with our accounting or legal obligations. We do not sell this information or use it for advertising.

You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID or Google Play account settings. Any refunds are handled by Apple or Google in accordance with their own policies.

Website Contact Forms

If you contact us through a contact form on our website, we will use the information you choose to provide (such as your name, email address, and message) only for the purpose of responding to your inquiry or providing support.

We do not sell, rent, or trade this information. We will retain it only as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your request, meet our legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by professional drivers and transportation professionals. It is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.

Data Security

While the App is designed to minimize or avoid collection of personal data, we take reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect any information that may be transmitted through our website (for example, contact form submissions). However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App, our practices, or legal requirements. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the App or website after any changes become effective means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle information, please contact us using the contact form on our website.
