# Google Search Console Setup Instructions

To get your website to show up when people search "Permit Path" on Google, follow these steps:

## Step 1: Verify Your Website with Google Search Console

1. Go to https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Click "Add Property"
3. Enter your website URL: `https://permitpathnav.com`
4. Choose verification method (HTML file upload recommended)
5. Google will provide a verification file - download it
6. Upload that file to your website's root directory
7. Click "Verify" in Google Search Console

## Step 2: Submit Your Sitemap

1. Once verified, go to "Sitemaps" in the left menu
2. Enter: `sitemap.xml`
3. Click "Submit"
4. This tells Google about all your pages

## Step 3: Request Indexing

1. Go to "URL Inspection" tool
2. Enter: `https://permitpathnav.com`
3. Click "Request Indexing"
4. Do this for your main pages:
   - https://permitpathnav.com/
   - https://permitpathnav.com/features.html
   - https://permitpathnav.com/pricing.html
   - https://permitpathnav.com/contact.html
   - https://permitpathnav.com/legal.html

## Step 4: Wait and Monitor

- Google typically indexes sites within a few days to a few weeks
- Check "Coverage" in Search Console to see if pages are indexed
- Monitor "Performance" to see search queries and rankings
