Hello,

Thank you for your interest in helping test PermitPath, a new route-building and navigation app designed specifically for oversize and permit-based trucking routes.

We are currently inviting a limited group of testers to help us evaluate the app before wider release. Your feedback will help improve routing accuracy, usability, and real-world performance for drivers.

Tester Rewards

Participants in the testing program may earn compensation based on participation, feedback quality, and completed testing tasks.

  • Testers may earn up to $1,000 during the beta program.
  • At the conclusion of the testing period, the Top 5 testers will receive $1,100 each based on overall contribution and participation.
  • Additional smaller rewards may be provided for valuable feedback, bug reports, and completed testing milestones.

How testers are evaluated

Compensation eligibility may be based on factors including:

  • Number of routes created or tested
  • Quality and usefulness of feedback submitted
  • Identification of bugs or improvement suggestions
  • Overall participation in the beta program

Important Program Terms

This is a voluntary beta testing opportunity. Participation does not create employment, contractor, or partnership status. Compensation is discretionary and based on participation and program criteria. Not all participants will qualify for monetary rewards.

All program rules, reward eligibility, and evaluation criteria may be updated as needed during the testing period to improve the testing process.

We appreciate your help in building a better navigation tool for drivers.
