Beta Testers

Requirements

  1. Must have an Android device.
  2. Must keep the app installed on your device for 14 days. On the 15th day you fill out a review and get paid.
  3. You must provide the email associated with your Google Play account. IMPORTANT: If you do not provide the email associated with your Google Play account, Google Play will not register you as an app tester.

Attention

This is a routing app for users to build routes on a map and then navigate the route they have built. The app could crash or glitch, which is normal in testing phases.

Google Play requires the app to be installed on a device for 14 days. We are not requiring you to use the app daily—only that you have it installed and fill out the questions on the 15th day.

Questions to answer on the 15th day:

  1. How many times did you use the app?
  2. Did the app crash?
  3. Did the app glitch? If so, explain what happened.
  4. Rate the app’s appearance (1–10).
  5. Rate the overall experience of using the app (1–10).
  6. Add comments.

Apply

How it works: PermitPath needs to be tested on 15 devices for 14 consecutive days in order to be released on the Google Play Store. We are looking for 20 people to test the app.

Once you apply, you will be added to the tester list and Google Play will email you a link to the app. The app must be installed on your device for 14 days. On the 15th day you will be prompted to answer a few questions about the app.

You will also receive an email to accept payment on the 15th day. Payments will be issued via Cash App, Venmo, or check.
